Detailing, done properly

A finish you can see your reflection in.

Detailing isn’t a rinse and a wipe. It’s a process. We strip the paint of every contaminant, correct the swirls and scratches, then lock in the gloss with a durable protective layer — inside and out. The result is a car that looks better than the day it left the lot.

Interior detail · full cabin refresh
STAGE 01

Wash & Decontaminate

Foam bath, hand wash, iron fallout removal, and a full clay treatment to leave the surface truly clean.

STAGE 02

Paint Correction

Machine polishing to cut swirls, oxidation, and scratches — restoring true depth and clarity to the paint.

STAGE 03

Protect & Coat

Sealant or ceramic coating plus a full interior refresh, so the shine and cleanliness actually last.
